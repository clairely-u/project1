How does Budget Buddy work?

To use the Budget Buddy, you'll start by entering your budget for your desired timeframe (you can do a day, week, month, etc!). Then, over the period of the time for which you've provided your budget amount, you'll enter how much money you spend in each category! Along the way, Budget Buddy can tell you how much of your budget remains, how much you've spent, and how much you've spent per category!
